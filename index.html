Welcome to Tesseroids

Tesseroids is a suite of open source programs for the forward modeling of gravitational fields in spherical coordinates. It can model the gravitational potential, acceleration, and gradient tensor. All programs run on the command-line and can be easily plugged into scripts and workflows.

The geometric element used in the modelling processes is a spherical prism, also called a tesseroid. However, Tesseroids also contains programs for modeling using right rectangular prisms, both in Cartesian and spherical coordinates.

Authors and Contributors

Tesseroids is developed by myself (Leonardo Uieda) in cooperation with prof. Carla Braitenberg.

It was first developed as my bachelor's thesis at the Universidade de Sao Paulo, Brazil. It has now made it's way into my PhD thesis at Observatorio Nacional, Brazil.
